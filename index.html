About

My approach to design is rooted in creating spaces that feel effortless, inviting, and in quiet harmony with nature. As an interior design master's student, I focus on the interplay of materiality, proportion, and spatial clarity to craft environments that balance refined aesthetics with intuitive functionality resulting in experiences that feel both timeless and deeply personal.
